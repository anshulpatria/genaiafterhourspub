What's a microsite?

A Microsite is a small standalone website. Usually 1-5 pages. Built for one specific thing
- a project, portfolio piece, event, or idea.


Different from a full website because it's focused. You can build one in an afternoon.

Activity 01  ·  15 min

Pick your thing.

"I want to build a microsite for [person] about [thing]. Give me 3 different directions I could take this."

For example I want to build a microsite for my sister who's a ceramic artist. Give me 3 different directions I could take this.

Paste that into any AI tool. Pick one direction. That's your brief.

Don't overthink it. The prompt can change. The idea can't stay vague.

Activity 02  ·  30 min

Build the first version.

"Build me a one-page site for [your thing]. Include [specific elements you want]. Keep it simple and clean."

For example Build me a one-page site for a freelance photographer. Include a fullscreen image grid, a short bio, and a contact form. Dark theme, minimal text.

Get something working. Doesn't need to be perfect. Just needs to exist.

Most tools let you iterate. First draft is for seeing if the idea works.

Stuck?  ·  anytime

Unstick yourself.

Hate the output? Say what you hate. "Less corporate" beats "try again".
Stuck on the idea? Find a site you like. Describe its layout.
Tool fighting you? Switch. Don't debug — just paste your prompt somewhere else.

Still lost - tap someone next to you..

You've got first version. Let's push it:

"Create a parallax effect between background and foreground layers."
"Add a click ripple effect when users tap buttons."
"Make cards tilt slightly with mouse movement."
"Add a floating gradient blob in the background."
"Create a slow looping shimmer across the page background."
"Add a custom cursor that grows on hover."
"Make images scale in softly as they enter view."
"Add a sticky section title that stays while you scroll."
"Animate numbers counting up when they appear on screen."
"Add a text highlight sweep under key words."
"Build a layered hero scene with depth on mouse move."
"Add a marquee line with looping words or short messages."
"Create a soft pulse behind the main CTA."
"Add a trail of light particles that follows the cursor."
"Make sections snap neatly into place while scrolling."
"Add a rotating 3D object in the hero section."
"Create a glass-style panel that reacts to hover."

"Add a reveal mask that uncovers images as you scroll."
"Create a spotlight effect that follows the cursor."
"Add one small surprise interaction to the final CTA."

One intentional change makes it less AI-generic.
